Privacy Policy

MagicK – Temple of the Goddesses  ·  Last updated: April 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how MagicK ("the app", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects user information. By using the app, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

MagicK is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect your name, email, or any directly identifiable personal data. The information we do collect includes:

  • Anonymous Device Identifier: randomly generated on the first launch to synchronize your Dharma balance with our server. It is not linked to your identity.
  • Progression Data: completed lessons, day streak, achievements unlocked. These are stored locally on your device using Hive.
  • Dharma Balance: the internal currency of the app, synchronized with our server to ensure consistency across sessions.
  • Anonymous Usage Events: app opens, sections visited, actions completed. These are used exclusively to improve the experience and do not include personal data.

2. Third Parties That Access Data

MagicK integrates the following third-party services, each with its own privacy policy:

  • RevenueCat — manages in-app purchases and subscriptions. Processes transaction information according to the RevenueCat Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob — advertising in the free version. May use device advertising identifiers according to the Google Privacy Policy. You can limit ad tracking from your iOS device settings (Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking).
  • Apple App Store — manages payments and subscriptions. Subject to Apple's terms.

3. Auto-Renewable Subscriptions

MagicK offers premium subscriptions (monthly and annual) processed exclusively through the Apple App Store. The subscription automatically renews unless you cancel it at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time from: iPhone Settings → your name → Subscriptions.

We do not directly store or process credit card information or payment methods; Apple handles all billing.

4. Local Storage

Most of your data (progression, preferences, study history) is saved locally on your device using Hive. This data is not transmitted to third parties and remains on your device.

5. Security

We use anonymous identifiers and do not collect sensitive data. Communication with our server is done via HTTPS. We do not store passwords or financial information.

6. Minors

MagicK is not directed at children under 17 and we do not intentionally collect information from minors. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided data, please contact us.

7. Your Rights

You can delete all your local data by uninstalling the application. To request the deletion of your anonymous identifier from our server, contact us at the email indicated below.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify you via an app update if the changes are significant. The "last updated" date at the top of this document reflects the current version.
